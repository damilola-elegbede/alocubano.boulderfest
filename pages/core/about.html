MISSION

We bring Cuban salsa to Boulder, Colorado through dance events, workshops, and festivals.

Our mission centers on bringing cuban salsa instructors and teachers to share traditional dance styles and movement with the Boulder community. We recognize that not everyone has the opportunity to travel to congresses so we're dedicated to bringing that experience to Boulder.
