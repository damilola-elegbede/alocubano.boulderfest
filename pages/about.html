MISSION

We bring Cuban culture to Boulder, Colorado through dance, music, and community.

Our mission centers on bringing authentic Cuban instructors and teachers to share traditional Cuban dance styles and movement with the Front Range community. We recognize that not everyone has the opportunity to travel to international congresses or experience Cuban culture firsthand, so we're dedicated to bringing that authentic experience directly to Boulder, creating an accessible gateway to the rich traditions of Cuban salsa and dance.
